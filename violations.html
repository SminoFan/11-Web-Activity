Violations Analysis

The top 10 most common violations in Los Angeles, represented in a horizontal bar graph. The most ticketed violation was 'Street Cleaning' with nearly 300,000 citations written over the period of the dataset.

The violation that carried the highest fine was a tie between 'Disabled Parking' and parking 3ft from a sidewalk ramp. Both violations carried fines of $352 dollars.

A feature that the project team would have implemented given more would have been allowing a user to query into the dataset a specific car model and have a bar graph return it's 10 most tickted offenses. An example of such is this Telsa bargraph.
