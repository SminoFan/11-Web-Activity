LAParking Citations

How to avoid getting parking tickets in LA? In this project, team members Ujwala, Kevin, Brandon and Emre looked at parking data in Los Angeles. We found a Los Angeles Parking Citations on and maintained by Kaggle.

We then cleaned the dataset by various means such as dropping NaN values, reducing redundancies in how values were written to the dataset. We divided the workload into four each member cleaning some part of the data for their analysis.

Afterwards each member positied a question that could be answered through analysis. What are the most common violations? What time of the day are most tickets handed out? Which part of LA was ticketed the most? Which make of car is the most ticketed?

The answers to these questions can be found on this website in the graphs generated through our analysis. Click visualizations, or the 'Analyses' drop down menu. A sample of the dataset can be found clicking the 'Data' tab.
